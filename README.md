# AutoCheck — Garage Management System

AutoCheck is a comprehensive, bilingual workspace designed for auto repair shops and garage managers to streamline their day-to-day operations. It replaces paperwork and disorganized spreadsheets with a unified system for tracking customers, vehicle service records, parts suppliers, work orders, and workshop finances.

---

## 🚘 Key Modules & Features

### 1. Dashboard (The Control Center)
A high-level summary of your garage's current status:
* **Today's Overview**: Instantly see how many customers, vehicles, active jobs, and suppliers are registered.
* **Job Progress Tracks**: Displays the count of repairs currently *Pending*, *In Progress*, or *Done*.
* **Service Analytics**: A visual chart highlighting the most serviced car brands in your garage.
* **Recent Activity**: A list of recently created work orders for quick access.

### 2. Customer Directory
A centralized address book for all your clients:
* Keep track of customer names, phone numbers, and email addresses.
* Access a customer's individual history page to see all vehicles they own and every repair job they have paid for.

### 3. Vehicle Fleet
Manage and inspect vehicles that visit your workshop:
* Log license plate numbers, manufacturer/model, optional VIN numbers, and custom descriptions (color, build year, trim details).
* Instantly view a vehicle's complete maintenance history to track recurring mechanical issues or past component upgrades.

### 4. Work Orders (Jobs)
Create and manage detailed repair sheets:
* **Job Tracking**: Log dates in and out, track vehicle statuses, and write mechanical notes.
* **Detailed Job Lines**: Log each individual task (e.g. "Oil & filter change") with its respective cost and supplier.
* **Payment Plans**: Record payments incrementally (e.g. deposit paid on day of check-in, balance cleared upon delivery).
* **Repair Sheets**: Renders a standardized, clean French repair sheet (*Fiche de Réparation*) with separate copies for the client and the garage records, prepared for signature and stamp.

### 5. Supplier Network
Manage the vendors and specialists that keep your workshop running:
* Group suppliers by type: parts wholesalers, specialized sub-contractors (e.g. body shops or electricians), and internal garage work.
* Trace supplier expenses and see which parts/labors were sourced from where.

### 6. Protected Finance Portal
A highly secure accounting overview designed for owners and managers:
* **Security Lock**: Shielded by a custom numeric PIN lock to keep financial details confidential when staff use the terminal.
* **Performance Indicators**: Monitor total billed revenue, collected payments, outstanding debts, and average ticket values.
* **Workshop Trends**: Graphical analyses tracking billing trends, payment breakdown ratios, and your top-paying clients.

### 7. Staff & Access Management
Set permissions for your workshop team:
* **Standard Staff**: Manage customers, cars, and create daily work orders.
* **Administrators**: Create staff logins, change user passwords, export/backup reports to CSV, and access financial summaries.

## 🔄 The AutoCheck Workflow

A typical day-to-day flow in the application follows these steps:

```
  [ New Client Arrives ] 
            │
            ▼
 1. Add Customer Info  ───►  2. Add Vehicle Details
                                      │
                                      ▼
                             3. Create Work Order (Job)
                             • Add lines of repairs
                             • Record advance deposits
                                      │
                                      ▼
                             4. Perform Repairs & Track Status
                                      │
                                      ▼
                             5. Complete Job & Collect Balance
                             • Print professional Fiche de Réparation
                             • Hand keys back to customer
```
